About

We dream of a prosperous India. We dream of a society that is not poor, an India which is literate, healthy and empowered. We dream of a country without any forms of vulnerability, exploitation and inefficiencies. We dream of choices, opportunities and collaboration.

Our mission is to enable innovations to impact the poor through social enterprise.At Villgro, we envision that once a model is established to transform innovative ideas into reality, a virtuous cycle will set in naturally, encouraging further innovation and wealth creation in rural areas.
